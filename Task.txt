Implement a shopping cart with the following functionality:

    Add products of different types
    Remove products
    If there are more than 2 product of the same type, their price should be discounted with 10%
    If the price of the whole shopping cart exceeds 100 (sum of all different product prices), total price should be discounted with 10%
    Calculate and print the shopping cart total price

Demonstrate a simple, but professional solution. No user interfaces are expected. Use technologies of your choice.
